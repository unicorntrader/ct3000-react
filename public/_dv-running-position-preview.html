Two ways to surface "you're managing an existing short" context on the day-row. Numbers below match yesterday's screenshot (CRWV cover 20 of an open -40, NBIS cover 30 of an open -60).

Problem: row tells you about the fill but not about the position it's part of. You see "Bought 20", but what was the position before? After?

Tradeoff: scannable column = quick "what's my exposure now". Costs ~120px column width; you'd want to drop something on small screens.
